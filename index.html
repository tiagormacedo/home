Tiago Macedo

I am an associate professor of mathematics at the Federal University of São Paulo (Unifesp), in Brazil. In the past, I have been a post-doctoral fellow at the University of Ottawa (in Canada), an assistant professor at the Federal University of Goiás (UFG-RC), and a Ph.D. student at the State University of Campinas (Unicamp).

Currently, I am teaching an undergraduate level class at Unifesp and am an editor of the journal Communications in Mathematics. More detailed information about me can be found in my Currículo Lattes and on my Github profile.

The best way to contact me is via email at tmacedo@unifesp.br.

Research

I am interested in applying homological methods to understand representation theory of Lie algebras, superalgebras and algebraic groups.
